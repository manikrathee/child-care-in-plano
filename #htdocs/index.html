Welcome to Little Angels Home Daycare!

Little Angels Home Daycare is a fully licensed home daycare that has been committed to providing a safe, caring and stimulating environment for infants and toddlers since 1995. Little Angels is based in Plano, Texas, northeast of the Dallas North Tollway and President George Bush Turnpike. We are conveniently located to serve Plano as well as the nearby communities of Allen, Frisco and Richardson. We look forward to meeting you and your child!
